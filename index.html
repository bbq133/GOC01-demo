THE PROBLEM核心问题

Platform Attribution Is Broken. You Just Can't See It Yet.平台归因早已失真，只是你还没看到。

Conflicting Credit重复的功劳认领

Every platform claims the same conversion. Meta says it drove the sale. Google says it did. TikTok agrees with itself. The total adds up to 150% of your actual revenue.每个平台都在抢同一个转化。Meta 说是它带来的，Google 说是自己的功劳，TikTok 也不甘示弱。加起来是实际收入的 150%。

Short-Window Bias短窗口偏差

Most platforms use 7-day click attribution. Upper-funnel channels like YouTube, display, and content campaigns get zero credit — even when they started the customer journey.大多数平台使用 7 天点击归因。YouTube、展示广告、内容营销等上中层渠道的贡献被完全忽略——即使用户旅程正是由它们开启。

Blind Budget Decisions盲目的预算决策

Without a unified standard, growth teams over-invest in channels that look good on paper and starve the channels that actually drive pipeline.没有统一标准，增长团队会过度投资那些「数据好看」的渠道，却饿死了真正驱动增长的渠道。

Which channels actually drive conversions?哪些渠道真正驱动了转化？

Estimate channel contribution under one unified logic. Reduce self-reported platform bias. Compare Meta, Google, TikTok, and DSP under the same standard.在统一逻辑下估算渠道贡献，减少平台自报偏差。用同一个标准对比 Meta、Google、TikTok 和 DSP。

Is your brand spend actually working?品牌广告的钱花得值不值？

Measure YouTube, display, content, and awareness campaigns with cross-device, long-window attribution. Connect early influence to later conversion using seeding rate and user value metrics.通过跨设备、长窗口归因衡量 YouTube、展示广告、内容营销的真实贡献。用种草率和用户价值指标，连接早期影响与后期转化。
