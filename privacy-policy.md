## 3. Our Role / 我们的角色

### 3.1 As a Data Processor / 作为数据处理者

When our Clients use the GOC AI Dashboard to collect and analyze their End Users' data, GOC acts as a **Data Processor** under GDPR and a **Service Provider** under CCPA. The Client is the **Data Controller** (or **Business** under CCPA) and determines the purposes and means of processing End User data.

当客户使用 GOC AI Dashboard 收集和分析其终端用户数据时，GOC 依据 GDPR 作为**数据处理者**、依据 CCPA 作为**服务提供者**。客户是**数据控制者**（或 CCPA 下的**企业**），负责决定终端用户数据的处理目的和方式。

### 3.2 As a Data Controller / 作为数据控制者

GOC acts as a **Data Controller** only for data we collect directly from our Clients during account registration, billing, and communication (e.g., business contact information, login credentials).

GOC 仅在从客户处直接收集的账户注册、计费和沟通数据（如商务联系信息、登录凭据）中作为**数据控制者**。

## 6. Data Storage and Security / 数据存储与安全

### 6.1 Infrastructure / 基础设施

All data is stored on **Google Cloud Platform** and processed using **Google BigQuery**. Our infrastructure is hosted in data centers that maintain industry-standard certifications (SOC 2, ISO 27001).

所有数据存储于 **Google Cloud Platform** 并使用 **Google BigQuery** 处理。我们的基础设施托管在具备行业标准认证（SOC 2、ISO 27001）的数据中心。

### 6.2 Security Measures / 安全措施

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

我们实施技术和组织措施保护数据，包括：

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
- Role-based access controls
- Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
- Secure credential management (passwords hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Data access logging and monitoring

- 传输中加密（TLS 1.2+）和静态加密（AES-256）
- 基于角色的访问控制
- 定期安全审计和漏洞评估
- 安全凭据管理（密码哈希存储，从不明文保存）
- 数据访问日志记录和监控

### 6.3 Data Retention / 数据保留

- **Client Account Data:** Retained for the duration of the account plus 30 days after termination, unless a longer retention period is required by law.
- **End User Data:** Retained in accordance with the Client's instructions and applicable Data Processing Agreement. Default retention is up to 365 days to support the full attribution window. Clients may request earlier deletion.

- **客户账户数据：** 在账户有效期内保留，账户终止后保留 30 天，法律另有要求的除外。
- **终端用户数据：** 根据客户指示和适用的数据处理协议保留。默认保留期最长为 365 天以支持完整归因窗口。客户可以请求提前删除。

Data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of the European Economic Area (EEA). When such transfers occur, we ensure adequate safeguards are in place through Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or other approved transfer mechanisms.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies / Cookie 与追踪技术

### 9.1 What Are Cookies / 什么是 Cookie

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide reporting information.

Cookie 是您访问的网站在您设备上放置的小型文本文件。它们被广泛用于使网站更高效地运行并提供报告信息。

### 9.2 Cookies We Use / 我们使用的 Cookie

**A. Strictly Necessary Cookies / 必要 Cookie**

Required for the Service to function. These cookies enable core features such as authentication, session management, and security. They cannot be disabled.

服务运行所必需的 Cookie。这些 Cookie 启用核心功能，如身份验证、会话管理和安全。它们不能被禁用。

| Cookie | Purpose / 用途 | Duration / 时长 |
|---|---|---|
| `session_id` | User session management / 用户会话管理 | Session / 会话期间 |
| `csrf_token` | Security — prevent cross-site request forgery / 安全 — 防止跨站请求伪造 | Session / 会话期间 |

**B. Analytics / Performance Cookies / 分析/性能 Cookie**

Help us understand how visitors interact with our website and Service dashboard.

帮助我们了解访客如何与我们的网站和服务看板互动。

| Cookie | Purpose / 用途 | Duration / 时长 |
|---|---|---|
| `_ga`, `_gid` | Google Analytics — aggregate usage statistics / Google Analytics — 聚合使用统计 | Up to 2 years / 最长 2 年 |

**C. Attribution Tracking (Client Websites) / 归因追踪（客户网站）**

When deployed on Client websites, the GOC tracking script uses first-party cookies to enable cross-device attribution and long-window measurement.

当部署在客户网站上时，GOC 追踪脚本使用第一方 Cookie 实现跨设备归因和长窗口衡量。

| Cookie | Purpose / 用途 | Duration / 时长 |
|---|---|---|
| `_goc_uid` | First-party user identifier for attribution / 用于归因的第一方用户标识 | Up to 365 days / 最长 365 天 |
| `_goc_src` | Source/campaign tracking / 来源/广告活动追踪 | Up to 365 days / 最长 365 天 |

These are **first-party cookies** set under the Client's domain, designed to provide accurate attribution measurement including on browsers with restrictive third-party cookie policies (such as Safari).

这些是在客户域名下设置的**第一方 Cookie**，旨在提供准确的归因衡量，包括在具有限制性第三方 Cookie 政策的浏览器（如 Safari）上。

### 9.3 Third-Party Cookies / 第三方 Cookie

Our Service may integrate with third-party platforms (Meta, Google, TikTok) that set their own cookies. These cookies are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy. GOC does not control third-party cookies.

我们的服务可能与第三方平台（Meta、Google、TikTok）集成，这些平台会设置自己的 Cookie。这些 Cookie 受各第三方隐私政策约束。GOC 不控制第三方 Cookie。

### 9.4 Managing Cookies / 管理 Cookie

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

您可以通过浏览器设置控制 Cookie：

- **Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences → Privacy
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

禁用某些 Cookie 可能会影响服务的功能。

**For End Users of Client websites:** Cookie consent and management on Client websites is the responsibility of the Client (Data Controller). Clients should implement appropriate cookie consent mechanisms for their End Users.

**对于客户网站的终端用户：** 客户网站上的 Cookie 同意和管理由客户（数据控制者）负责。客户应为其终端用户实施适当的 Cookie 同意机制。
